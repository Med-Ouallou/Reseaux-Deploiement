A 20-Year Journey
HISTORY
& VERSIONS

From its humble 2004 debut, Ubuntu has grown into the world's most popular Linux distribution, powering desktops, servers, and the cloud.

2004
Ubuntu 4.10 · Warty Warthog
The very first Ubuntu release, founded by Mark Shuttleworth and Canonical, based on Debian.
2006
Ubuntu 6.06 LTS · Dapper Drake
First Long-Term Support release, establishing Ubuntu's enterprise credibility.
2011
Ubuntu 11.04 · Natty Narwhal
Introduced Unity desktop, marking a bold UI redesign aimed at touch and convergence.
2018
Ubuntu 18.04 LTS · Bionic Beaver
Returned to GNOME desktop. Snap packages introduced widely for app distribution.
2024
Ubuntu 24.04 LTS · Noble Numbat
Latest LTS with GNOME 46, improved hardware support, and next-gen cloud tooling.
